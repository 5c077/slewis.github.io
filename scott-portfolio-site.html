About Me

Professional Summary

Innovative and detail-oriented individual contributor with 10+ years of experience developing cutting-edge bioinformatics solutions for emerging NGS/HTS technologies. I earned a Ph.D. in Biology & Biomedical Sciences (program in Genetics & Genomics) from Washington University School of Medicine and continuously hone extensive experience in computational biology, statistics, and machine learning. I specialize in translating complex biological data into actionable insights through advanced analytical workflows, custom software development, and transformative visualizations.

My expertise spans single-cell RNA sequencing, spatial transcriptomics, epigenetic reprogramming, and high-performance computing. I have a proven track record of driving discovery among interdisciplinary teams, from academic research to consulting with enterprise clients and startups.
